Practical tactics for getting students to actually show up — and stay.
How to Get People to Show Up
  • 1
    Lead with what's free. Free soft drinks, free craft item, free snacks — put it in the subject line.
  • 2
    Run the survey first. Asking "what do YOU want?" before announcing events creates ownership.
  • 3
    Scarcity framing. "Only 12 spots" consistently outperforms open-ended invites.
  • 4
    Knock on doors. A personal invite beats any email. Two minutes of contact is worth three email blasts.
  • 5
    Post photos after events. Photos on the noticeboard normalise participation and build FOMO.
  • 6
    Wednesday evenings or Sunday afternoons. Highest-turnout windows in student accommodation.
